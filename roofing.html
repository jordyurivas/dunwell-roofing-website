Architectural shingles (also called dimensional shingles) are a premium upgrade from standard 3-tab shingles. They are thicker, more durable, and add a distinctive layered look to your home that boosts curb appeal and resale value.

  • Thicker and more durable than standard shingles

  • Longer lifespan than 3-tab shingles

Flat Roofing

TPO Membrane Roofing

At Dunwell we specialize in TPO reroofs, TPO repair, and coatings. TPO is a vinyl membrane flat roofing material that requires specialized tools and knowledge to install. It typically has a higher price point than standard flat roof torch down, but also comes with a better lifespan of around 30 years.

  • 30-year lifespan
  • Vinyl membrane material -- durable and weather resistant
  • TPO reroofs, repairs, and coatings available
  • Requires specialized tools and trained installers
  • Suitable for residential and commercial flat roofs
